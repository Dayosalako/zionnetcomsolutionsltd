Building Nigeria's Digital Infrastructure

Zion Netcom Solutions Limited was founded in 2023 with a singular mission: to bridge the digital infrastructure gap across Nigerian institutions and communities. What began as a small network consultancy in Kaduna has grown into a full-service technology company delivering enterprise networking, custom web applications, and large-scale ICT deployments.

Our team combines deep expertise in telecommunications engineering with modern software development practices. We have deployed networks for federal universities, technical education boards, and vocational schools — while simultaneously building web platforms that serve thousands of users across community, logistics, and education sectors.

Looking ahead, we are actively developing capabilities in AI-powered surveillance, solar-powered ICT infrastructure, and industrial IoT — positioning Zion Netcom at the forefront of Africa's next-generation technology landscape.

Evolution

Our Timeline

2023

Foundation

Zion Netcom Solutions Limited incorporated in Kaduna, Nigeria. Initial focus on network consulting and small-scale ISP infrastructure.

2024

First Institutional Contract

Secured first major ICT deployment contract with a federal educational institution, establishing our campus network expertise.

2024

Software Division Launch

Expanded into full-stack web development with the launch of Biu Forum — our first major community platform built on Google Cloud.

2025

Multi-Campus Expansion

Deployed campus-wide networks for Federal University Wukari and Bauchi State University, Yuli Campus. Starlink integration capabilities added.

2024

NBTE Partnership

Began comprehensive network management and support for the National Board for Technical Education — our largest ongoing institutional contract.

2023-2026

Product Portfolio Growth

Launched SchoolOS (school management system), ZionDrive (enterprise file management), and began development of DND Logistics and Harmony Welfare platforms.

2025-2026

Future Tech Roadmap

Active R&D in AI surveillance, solar ICT infrastructure, and industrial IoT. Organization Management System and Identification System in development.
